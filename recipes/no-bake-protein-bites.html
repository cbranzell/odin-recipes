No Bake Protein Bites

Description

These delicious protein bites are easy to make, loaded with nutritious ingredients, and taste great! They make a perfect quick snack. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup natural peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup protein powder of choice
  • 1/3 cup flaxseed meal
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 tsp. cinnamon
  • 1 Tbl. chia seeds
  • 1 Tbl. mini chocolate chips

Steps

  1. In a food processor, add in peanut butter, honey, vanilla, protein powder, flaxseed meal, oats, cinnamon, and chia seeds.
  2. Pulse together until well combined.
  3. Add in chocolate chips and pulse a few more times.
  4. Use a medium cookies scoop or your hands to grab dough and roll into 10 balls.
  5. Place in an airtight container and store in the fridge for up to 1 week.
