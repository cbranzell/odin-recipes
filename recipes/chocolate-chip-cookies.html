Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This is my take on the classic, family-favorite. Every family has their own favorite recipe for chocolate chip cookies, and mine is no different.

Here I've made a few adjustments to my family's favorite recipe to take it up to the next. I hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1/2 lb. unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chip
  • 1 cup milk chocolate chunks
  • flakey sea salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
  2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, combine flour, salt, and baking soda. Set aside.
  3. In a seperate bowl, cream butter, sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla.
  4. Beat in eggs, one at a time.
  5. Gradually mix in dry ingredients, mixing until combined.
  6. Stir in chocolate.
  7. Roll into balls and place on ungreased cookie sheet, 2 inches apart.
  8. Bake for 9-11 minutes until golden brown.
  9. Immediately after removing from oven, sprinkly flakey salt over top of cookies.
