Mom's Favorite Brownies

Description

This family recipe is always a hit! Whether you prefer your brownies on the fudgey, cakey, or chewy side, you're going to love these!

Ingredients

  • 2 packages (8 oz. total) Baker's unsweetend chocolate
  • 1 cup butter
  • 5 eggs
  • 3 cups sugar
  • 1 tsp. vanilla
  • 1 1/2 cuprs all purpose flour
  • 2 cups chopped walnuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
  2. Melt chocolate and butter in saucepan over very low heat, stirring constantly. Cool slightly.
  3. Beat eggs, sugar, and vanilla in a large mixer bowl on high speed for 10 minutes.
  4. Beat in choclate mixture on low speed.
  5. Add flour, beating in just to blend.
  6. (optional) Stir in walnuts.
  7. Spread in a greased 13x9" pan.
  8. Bake for 35-40 minutes until a cake tester inserted into the center comes out almost clean. Cool in pan.
